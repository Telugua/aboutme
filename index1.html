Current

Plans

I'm not sure exactly what I want to do with my life. I enjoy coding obviously, so I think

  1. Software Engineering
  2. Database Management
  3. Game Development
would all be fun areas to go into. My emphasis in security will help me with pretty much any field. For now, I plan on graduating from Northwest Missouri State University with my Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science, then look for a job with good work/life balance. This way I can start a family while also getting paid for doing what I love. If I feel super motivated later in life, I may return to school to work on a Master's degree in some specific area of computer science.

Background

I'm from Gladstone, Missouri which is a suburb of Kansas City. I went to Oak Park High School and participated in pretty much all the bands and choirs there. I took my first programming class my last semester high school and fell in love with it. That is how I decided on my major.

As for prior experience, I worked as a Quality Assurance Intern for American Century Investments this past summer. My job consisted mostly of developing and running automated tests. I was also responsible for investigating and reporting failures. I got to be part of a scrum team and learn about Agile development. I've accepted an internship for this coming summer as a Software Engineer Intern at Principal Financial Group in Des Moines, Iowa. I am very excited for this opportunity and cannot wait to learn from more real world.
